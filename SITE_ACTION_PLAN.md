# Modern Marketing Site Action Plan (Inspired by brentozar.com & sqlbi.com)

## 1. Discovery & Analysis
- Review homepage, navigation, and key sections of both sites.
- Identify shared elements: hero, services, blog, resources, testimonials, CTAs, footer, etc.
- Note unique features/styles from each to blend.

## 2. Site Structure & Navigation
- Plan main navigation: Home, About, Services, Blog, Resources, Contact.
- Decide on dropdowns or mega-menu if needed.

## 3. Homepage Layout
- Hero section with headline, subheadline, CTA (Brent Ozar style).
- Services/offerings grid (SQLBI style).
- Featured blog posts or resources.
- Testimonials or client logos.
- Newsletter signup/lead magnet.
- Footer with links, social, and legal.

## 4. Core Pages
- About: Team, mission, credentials.
- Services: Detailed offerings, possibly with icons or cards.
- Blog: List of posts, categories/tags, featured post.
- Resources: Free downloads, webinars, tools, or guides.
- Contact: Form with spam protection, business info.

## 5. Reusable Components
- Navbar (responsive, sticky)
- Hero/CTA blocks
- Service cards
- Blog post cards
- Testimonial slider or grid
- Newsletter signup form
- Footer

## 6. Branding & Visuals
- Blend color palette and typography from both sites, using your brand colors (#008F55, #CFB53B).
- Use professional, clean imagery and icons.

## 7. Content Migration/Creation
- Draft or migrate content for each section/page.
- Prepare sample blog posts, case studies, testimonials.

## 8. Enhancements
- Add search, filtering, or tagging for blog/resources.
- Integrate analytics, SEO, and accessibility best practices.
