# Step 1: Discovery & Analysis — BI/Analytics Focus

## Key Elements from brentozar.com
- **Hero Section:**
  - Clear headline, subheadline, and strong CTA ("Get Help Now").
  - Personal photo and credibility statement.
- **Services/Offerings:**
  - Simple, direct service cards (Consulting, Training, Resources).
  - Emphasis on expertise and results.
- **Blog/Content:**
  - Recent posts, popular posts, and search.
  - Categories for easy navigation.
- **Testimonials:**
  - Client quotes and logos for social proof.
- **Newsletter Signup:**
  - Prominent, value-driven CTA.
- **Footer:**
  - Social links, copyright, legal.

## Key Elements from sqlbi.com
- **Hero Section:**
  - Clean, modern intro with value proposition.
- **Services/Training:**
  - Grid of offerings (Courses, Books, Tools, Consulting).
  - Visual icons and short descriptions.
- **Featured Content:**
  - Highlighted articles, videos, and resources.

- **Footer:**

## Shared/Recommended Sections for BI/Analytics Site
- Hero section with headline, subheadline, CTA, and credibility (photo or logo).
- Services grid: Consulting, Training, Data Strategy, Custom Solutions.
- Featured blog posts/resources: Latest insights, guides, or tools.
- Testimonials/client logos: Social proof from BI/analytics clients.
- Newsletter signup: "Get BI insights in your inbox."
- Footer: Social, legal, contact info.

## Unique Features to Blend
- Personal touch (Brent Ozar): Photo, direct language, credibility.
- Modern, visual grid (SQLBI): Icons, clean cards, event highlights.
- Search and categories for blog/resources.
- Lead magnets: Free guides, webinars, or tools.

## Must-Have Homepage Sections (BI/Analytics Focus)
